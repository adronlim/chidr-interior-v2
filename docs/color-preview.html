Discover our approach

CH iDesign & Renovation brings together interior design, space planning and renovation into a single, coherent practice. We work across Penang — with private homes, boutique commercial spaces and offices — always guided by the same principle: restraint in material, richness in experience.

Every project begins with listening. We study how a family moves through their home, how a team inhabits their workspace, how light shifts across a room at different hours. From that understanding, we build interiors that earn their place quietly and lastingly.
